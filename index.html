Hard reset Samsung Galaxy Core 2

Today I will tell you how to reset Samsung Galaxy Core 2. I have seen many people who get their phone locked and can’t find any way to recover it.And then spending money to get it unlocked. But it is very simple and easy to recover your phone and it is free of cost. Yes, you heard it right, you don’t have spend even a single penny. And believe me, there are NO risks involved in doing that.

The method is known as Hard Reset. It can also be done if your phone is getting to slow due to virus or you just want to wipe out all the data from your phone. But the major point to be noted is that ALL YOUR DATA WILL BE ERASED. Your phone will restore factory settings. Please backup your personal data, if possible.

The process is nearly the same for all the Android phones except the buttons to be pressed which may be different in different phones. I am telling you the step wise procedure for hard resetting SAMSUNG GALAXY CORE 2.

Here are the steps…

Firstly, Switch off your phone and pull out the battery. Take out the sim card and the memory card from your phone.

Then, press and hold the VOLUME UP button + HOME button + POWER button. A black screen with various options will appear.

Now, using the Volume Buttons, select the wipe data/factory reset option. Then, press the Power button to confirm.

That’s all. Now your phone will boot up and your phone will be restored to factory settings.
